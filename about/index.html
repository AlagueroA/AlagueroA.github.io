Personal Skills

Ambitious

From a young age, I have been captivated by the mysteries of space. The desire to study them emerged early on, evolving into a dream that has guided my academic journey. Aware of the intense competition, I have consistently sought out the most favorable conditions to realize this aspiration. While my efforts have yielded promising results, the journey is still ongoing. With a pragmatic approach and a forward-looking mindset, I am committed to making meaningful contributions to science, all while pursuing a passion that deeply inspires me.

Open-minded

I believe I have a lot to learn from the others, which is why I always welcome advice, feedback, and constructive criticism as essential tools for refining my work and adapt to the evolving landscape of astrophysics. Beyond my own development, I am passionate about fostering a culture of inclusivity and social justice within the academic community. In this context, I prioritize active listening and have earned a reputation as a trustworthy and reliable colleague and friend.

Versatile
